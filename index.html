About

A place to find out about life after having children, the tips and tricks that you really should know, besides the usual advice you're bombarded with as a new parent. This is a collection of tips that I've learnt or knowledge that other parents have bestowed on me. I am a mother of one beautiful toddler, who is soon to be 2! I do not claim to be an expert in the art of parenting, this site is just a collection of things that might help to make life easier when thrust into the magical, yet tiring world of parenting.

Parenting Tips

When you're expecting with a child, you get a miriad of advice from well meaning family members as well as other seasoned parents. This is all lovely, but your child is/will be unique and what works for one parent/child may or may not work for you. I would advise to listen to all advice given and then do what you want for your child. My husband and I went against some of the prescibed advice out there for parents such as never make up formular feeds in advance - i'll explain why later.

My fitst tip is about baby vests - something you may or may not know; the folded over shoulder parts are for when your darling child has had an unfortunate poo-narmi event and instead of cutting them out of the vest or pulling it over their head and running the risk of more poo everywhere, these shoulder parts are designed to be folded back so that the vest can be slid down the arms and off over the legs. No one tells you these little tricks! It will save you from a bigger mess - trust me!
